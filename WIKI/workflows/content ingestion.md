# Content Ingestion #workflow

## Key Insight
Knowledge only compounds if input is processed, not just stored.

---

## What it is

The process of converting raw content into structured knowledge.

---

## Steps

1. Capture
- clip article or video
- store in raw/web

2. Process (LLM)
- summarize
- extract key ideas
- identify concepts

3. Structure
- update existing notes
- or create new ones

4. Link
- raw → wiki
- wiki → raw
- connect related notes

5. Output
- generate posts
- generate ideas
- feed apps

---

## Rules

- never leave raw unprocessed
- always link both ways
- prioritize concepts over summaries

---

## Why it matters

- prevents knowledge loss
- enables automation
- creates compounding system
