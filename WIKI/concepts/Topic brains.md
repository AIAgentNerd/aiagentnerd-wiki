# Topic Brains #concept #product

## Key Insight
Each topic should have its own evolving knowledge system, not just notes.

---

## What it is

A Topic Brain is a self-contained knowledge system focused on a specific topic.

It includes:
- raw inputs (articles, videos)
- structured wiki knowledge
- outputs (posts, insights, ideas)

---

## Mental Model

Topic = Mini knowledge system

raw → wiki → outputs

---

## Why it matters

- enables deep understanding over time
- supports automation (agents)
- creates reusable intelligence
- foundation for TaskNerd product
