## USER

Help me study vocabulary: write a sentence for me to fill in the blank, and I'll try to pick the correct option.

## ASSISTANT

Here’s a vocabulary practice sentence. Choose the word that best fits the blank.

**Sentence:**  
The scientist’s hypothesis was ______, but after months of experimentation she found evidence that supported it.

**Options:**  
A) dubious  
B) ubiquitous  
C) trivial  
D) ubiquitous  

(Note: Options B and D are the same on purpose—only one is correct; pick the best fit.)  

Your turn! Fill in the blank with the letter of the correct choice.
